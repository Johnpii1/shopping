Sneaker Care

Posted on Nov 11, 2025 by Johnpaul

Sneakers are more than just footwear — they’re a fashion statement. To keep them looking fresh and lasting longer, a little care goes a long way.

1. Clean Regularly: Wipe your sneakers after each wear with a damp cloth. For deeper cleaning, use mild soap and a soft brush to remove stains and dirt.

2. Air Dry Only: Never put your sneakers in direct sunlight or a dryer. Let them air-dry naturally to prevent color fading or cracking.

3. Use Shoe Trees: Shoe trees help maintain the shape of your sneakers and prevent creases, especially for leather or canvas pairs.

4. Protect with Spray: A good waterproof or stain-repellent spray can save your sneakers from unexpected spills and rain.

5. Rotate Your Pairs: Don’t wear the same pair every day — rotating gives them time to breathe and last longer.

With simple, consistent care, your sneakers will stay clean, comfortable, and ready to complement any outfit.
